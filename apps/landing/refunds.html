Refund & Purchase Policy

Last updated 21 July 2026

AfriStage sells coins — a digital currency you buy to send gifts to creators during live streams. This policy explains what you are buying, when a purchase can be refunded, and how we handle disputes and chargebacks. It applies to every coin purchase made through the AfriStage app.

What you are buying

  • Coins are a digital good, credited to your wallet immediately after your payment is confirmed.
  • Prices are shown in your local currency at checkout and are final at the moment of purchase.
  • Coins have no cash value, are not a deposit, and cannot be exchanged back into money by users.

Refunds on coin purchases

Because coins are delivered instantly, purchases are generally non-refundable once the coins are credited. We will, however, refund a purchase in these cases:

  • Unauthorised or duplicate charge — you were charged for a purchase you did not make, or charged twice for the same order.
  • Coins not delivered — your payment succeeded but the coins never appeared in your wallet, and our records confirm the charge.
  • Provable technical error — a bug on our side caused the wrong amount to be charged.

Refund requests must be made within 14 days of the charge. Approved refunds are returned to your original payment method and any coins from that purchase that are still unspent are removed from your wallet.

Gifts to creators

Coins spent as gifts are final. Once you send a gift to a creator, the coins are transferred and cannot be refunded — the creator has earned them. If you believe a gift was sent in error or through account misuse, contact us and we will investigate.

Disputes & chargebacks

If you do not recognise a charge, please contact us first — we can usually resolve it faster than your bank. If you open a chargeback with your bank or card provider:

  • The disputed purchase is marked disputed and the corresponding coins are reversed from your wallet on our ledger, even if they have already been spent.
  • We may temporarily restrict purchases on the account while the dispute is resolved.
  • We respond to card-network and processor disputes with our transaction records as evidence.

Payments are processed by Paystack (African currencies) and Stripe (international cards). AfriStage never sees or stores your full card details.

Creator payouts

Earnings withdrawn by creators follow the payout terms in the creator agreement. A payout that fails or is rejected is returned to the creator's earnings balance; it is never silently lost.

Contact us

For any refund request, unrecognised charge, or billing question, reach us with the email on your account and the approximate date and amount of the charge:

support@afristage.live

We aim to respond within two business days.
