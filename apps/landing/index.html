The money loop

Go live. Get gifted. Get paid.

No ads to chase, no algorithm to game. Fans turn coins into gifts, gifts into your earnings, earnings into cash — every step on a ledger that always balances.

1

Open a music, comedy, or talk room in seconds. Set the title, pick a language, hit start.

3

You earn coins

Most of every gift lands in your earnings instantly. Watch the top-gifter board climb in real time.

4

Cash out

Request a payout to your bank or mobile money. Reviewed, tracked, and paid — with a receipt.

Money you can trust

A creator economy that adds up.

A platform that handles real money has to be honest about it. Here's how we keep yours safe.

▦

Every coin accounted for

A double-entry ledger sits under every gift and payout — debits always equal credits, checked continuously. Your earnings are never a guess.

◷

Payouts you can see

Track each request from review to paid. Some earnings are held briefly for fraud and payment checks — and we tell you exactly why.

⚇

Moderation from day one

Real review of reports, fast room takedowns, and fraud signals on the payout queue — so the stage stays safe to perform on.
